📊 Frequency Analyser

Paste the encrypted text below. The tool counts each letter's frequency. In English, E appears most (~13%). The tallest bar is likely E encrypted — its position tells you the shift!

AZ
How to use this to find the shift:
1. Find the tallest bar — that letter is the most frequent in the cipher.
2. In English, the most common letter is E.
3. Count the positions from E to your tallest bar = the shift.
Example: tallest bar is H → H is 3 steps after E → shift is 3.
